Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2025

Overview

Orchestrator is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide and improve the app experience.

Data We Collect

Orchestrator may collect the following types of data:

  • Device Identifier: Used for app functionality and sync purposes. This data is not linked to your identity and is not used for tracking.
  • Performance Data: Crash logs and diagnostic information to help us improve app stability. This data is not linked to your identity.

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing core app functionality
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Diagnosing and fixing bugs

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties. We do not use any tracking domains or third-party analytics services.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. Any data transmitted for crash reporting is anonymized and cannot be linked back to you.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of crash reporting through system preferences

Children's Privacy

Orchestrator does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@orchest.org.
